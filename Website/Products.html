Oppo Realme U1 smartphone will be powered by MediaTek Helio P70 chipset. It is a new device under Realme’s U series. The company is touting that the phone will be the world’s first handset to be powered by the newly launched MediaTek Helio P70 processor. The phone is an Amazon India exclusive. It has a waterdrop notch design which gives it a sleek look, with slim edges. It will have an edge to edge display with a high screen to body ratio. The Helio P70 processor offers a boost over its predecessor so users will get faster processing.

Realme C1 was launched in September 2018 & runs on Android 8 OS. The Smartphone has a built in fingerprint sensor as the primary security feature, along with the host of connectivity options in terms of 3G, 4G, GPS, Wifi Bluetooth capabilities. Priced at Rs. 8999 the phone is available with 16 GB of internal storage.

The Smartphone is powered by 1.8 GHz Octa core Qualcomm Snapdragon 450 Processor. A 2 GB of RAM ensures phone runs smoothly even the most memory intensive applications & still shows no signs of lag. 16 GB of internal storage can be expanded via a microSD card.
Realme 2 Pro 128GB was launched in September 2018 & runs on Android 8 OS. The Smartphone is available in three color options i.e. Ice Lake, Blue Ocean, Black Sea & has a built in fingerprint sensor as the primary security feature, along with the host of connectivity options in terms of 3G, 4G, GPS, Wifi, NFC Bluetooth capabilities. The phone is available with 128GB of internal storage.

Realme 2 was launched in September 2018 & runs on Android 8.1 OS. The Smartphone is available in three color options i.e. Blue, Black, Red & has a built in fingerprint sensor as the primary security feature, along with the host of connectivity options in terms of 3G, 4G, GPS, Wifi Bluetooth capabilities. The phone is available with 64 GB of internal storage.

The Smartphone is powered by 1.8 GHz Octa core Qualcomm SDM450 Snapdragon 450 Processor. A 4 GB of RAM & Adreno 506 graphics processor ensures phone runs smoothly even the most memory intensive applications & still shows no signs of lag. 64 GB of internal storage can be expanded to 256 GB via a microSD card.
RealMe 1 128GB is announced to be launched in May 2018 & will run on Android 8.1 OS. The Smartphone will be available in two color options i.e. Diamond Black, Solar Red & will have a host of connectivity options in terms of 3G, 4G, Wifi Bluetooth capabilities. The phone will be launched at a price of Rs. 14990 & will come with 128GB of internal storage.

The Smartphone will be powered by 2 GHz Octa core Mediatek Helio P60 Processor. A 6 GB of RAM & Mali-G72 MP3 graphics processor will ensure phone runs smoothly even the most memory intensive applications with no signs of lag. 128GB of internal storage will be open for expansion upto 256 GB via a microSD card.
